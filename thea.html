Trivia

There isn't much known about Theresa publically, even her name seems to be made up. She earns money by singing and playing the piano in small bars.

One of the few people ever having a longer conversation with her is John. Even though you might not expect, Thea loves technology. She voiced his robot in return to John teaching her basic computer skills.

When talking to her, John felt a little uneasy. She knows suspiciously little about smartphones, for example. Yet, if he mentions in depth things like the Neumann architecture, she can tell him everything about it. Jokingly, he once asked her whether she was born in another century, to which she agreed. Since then, he is absolutely sure that she must be a vampire. But John might be a little crazy anyway...
